Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 14, 2025

Peoplo our company is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application, including when interacting via Google Assistant App Actions.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information unless explicitly provided by the user. When you interact with our app via App Actions, we may process voice commands and actions using Google Assistant infrastructure, but we do not store or access your voice recordings.

Types of data we may access include:

  • Voice action intents (e.g., "View Attendance", "Apply for Leave")
  • Non-personal usage data (e.g., command triggered, success/failure status)
  • App usage logs (only for performance monitoring and crash reports)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Perform the requested action (e.g., navigate to a specific screen)
  • Improve user experience by handling commands accurately
  • Ensure app performance and fix bugs

We do not use your information for:

  • Marketing or advertising
  • Sharing with third-party vendors

3. Permissions

Our app may request the following permissions:

  • Microphone: To process voice commands locally (via in-app voice recognition, not stored or shared)
  • Internet: To enable App Actions integration and basic app functionality

4. Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal data with third parties. However, your interaction with Google Assistant is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

We do not retain any personally identifiable data from your voice commands. All interactions are processed in real-time and discarded afterward.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
